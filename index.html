Course Overview

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is an undergraduate program available in 3‑year (Regular), 4‑year Honours, and 4‑year Honours with Research variants under the NEP framework. It equips students with fundamental to advanced computing skills through theory, lab sessions, project work, and industry internships covering programming, databases, web technologies, networking, and software engineering.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common queries from prospective and current students.

What is CPGEE and how do I apply for MCA admission?

CPGEE (Cotton University Post Graduate Entrance Examination) is the primary entrance test for MCA admission, conducted by Cotton University usually in May–June. It is an offline OMR-based exam with 200 MCQ questions covering Mathematics, Computer Fundamentals & Programming, DBMS, Networking & OS, and General Aptitude — totalling 200 marks with −0.25 negative marking. Apply through the official Cotton University admission portal.

What is the eligibility for BCA admission at Cotton University?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Class XII) from a recognized board in any stream with Mathematics as one of the subjects. A minimum aggregate of 45% marks is required for General category and 40% for SC/ST/OBC candidates. Admission is through class XII (12th) marks.

Is the MCA admission through NIMCET or CPGEE or CUET?

No, Cotton University primarily uses CPGEE (its own entrance) for MCA admissions. check the official university portal for each year's notification.

What is the CPGEE syllabus and exam pattern?

CPGEE has 5 sections: (A) Mathematics — 50 marks covering calculus, algebra, probability; (B) Computer Fundamentals & Programming — 60 marks covering C, data structures, algorithms; (C) DBMS — 30 marks covering SQL, normalization, transactions; (D) Networking & OS — 30 marks covering TCP/IP, process management; (E) General Aptitude — 30 marks covering reasoning & verbal ability. Total: 200 marks, 200 MCQs, 2 hours, offline OMR mode, −0.25 negative marking. Full syllabus PDF is available in the Syllabus section.

What is the annual fee structure for BCA, B.Sc CS, and MCA?

Being a central university, Cotton University maintains affordable fee structures: BCA & B.Sc CS: approximately ₹8,000–12,000 per annum for tuition. MCA: approximately ₹14,000–18,000 per annum. Additional charges include laboratory, library, and sports fees. Scholarship-holders may receive significant waivers. Please visit the official Cotton University website for updated fee details.

Does the department offer placement assistance?

Yes! The department has an active Placement & career counseling Cell conducting mock interviews, resume-building workshops, and coding bootcamps. Companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, and various startups recruit from campus. The placement rate for 2023–24 was approximately 85% for eligible students.

What programming languages and technologies are taught?

The curriculum covers: C, C++, Java, Python, PHP, JavaScript for programming; MySQL, MongoDB, Oracle for databases; HTML/CSS, React basics for web; AWS & Google Cloud for cloud computing; TensorFlow & scikit-learn for ML; Android for mobile development. The curriculum is revised annually to stay current with industry demands.
