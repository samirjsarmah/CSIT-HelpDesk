Course Overview

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is an undergraduate program available in 3‑year (Regular), 4‑year Honours, and 4‑year Honours with Research variants under the NEP framework. It equips students with fundamental to advanced computing skills through theory, lab sessions, project work, and industry internships covering programming, databases, web technologies, networking, and software engineering.

Intake20 Seats

Course Overview

B.Sc. Computer Science offers a rigorous science‑based curriculum with strong emphasis on mathematical foundations, algorithm analysis, AI, machine learning, and computational theory. Available in Regular (3-year/6-semester), Honours (4-year/8-semester), and Honours with Research variants under NEP. Students gain deep conceptual understanding alongside practical programming skills suitable for research or industry.

MCA is a 2‑year postgraduate program that prepares students for senior technical and managerial roles in the IT industry. Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common queries from prospective and current students.

What is CPGEE and how do I apply for MCA admission?

CPGEE (Cotton University Post Graduate Entrance Examination) is the primary entrance test for MCA admission, conducted by Cotton University usually in May–June. It is an offline OMR-based exam with 200 MCQ questions covering Mathematics, Computer Fundamentals & Programming, DBMS, Networking & OS, and General Aptitude — totalling 200 marks with −1 negative marking and +4 for correct answer. Apply through the official Cotton University admission portal.

What is the eligibility for BCA admission at Cotton University?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 standard pass with minimum 60% aggregate marks or equivalent grade points with pass marks in Mathematics/Applied Mathematics. candidates. Admission is through class XII (12th) marks.

Is the MCA admission through NIMCET or CPGEE or CUET?

No, Cotton University primarily uses CPGEE (its own entrance) for MCA admissions. Check the official university portal for each year's notification.

What is the CPGEE syllabus and exam pattern?

CPGEE has 5 sections: (A) Mathematics — 50 marks covering calculus, algebra, probability; (B) Computer Fundamentals & Programming — 60 marks covering C, data structures, algorithms; (C) DBMS — 30 marks covering SQL, normalization, transactions; (D) Networking & OS — 30 marks covering TCP/IP, process management; (E) General Aptitude — 30 marks covering reasoning & verbal ability. Total: 200 marks, 200 MCQs, 2 hours, offline OMR mode, −1 negative marking.

What is the annual fee structure for BCA, B.Sc. CS, and MCA?

Being a central university, Cotton University maintains affordable fee structures: BCA (Self Financed Course): approcimately 22,500 (FIRST SEM), ₹14,500 EVEN SEM & ₹19,200 ODD SEM & B.Sc CS: approximately ₹8,000 . MCA: approximately ₹14,000–18,000 per annum. Additional charges include laboratory, library, and sports fees. Please visit the official Cotton University website for updated fee details.

Does the department offer placement assistance?

Yes! The department has an active Placement & career counseling Cell conducting mock interviews, resume-building workshops, and coding bootcamps. Companies like TCS, ICICI BANK, HDFC BANK, OIL INDIA and various startups recruit from campus.

What programming languages and technologies are taught?

The curriculum covers: C, C++, Java, Python, PHP, JavaScript for programming; MySQL, MongoDB, Oracle for databases; HTML/CSS, React basics for web; AWS & Google Cloud for cloud computing; TensorFlow & scikit-learn for ML; Android for mobile development.
